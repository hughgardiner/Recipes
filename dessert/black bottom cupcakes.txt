Black Bottom Cupcakes

makes ~ 2 dozen

preheat to 350 

1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

1 cup water
1/2 cup corn/veg oil
1 Tablespoon distilled white vinegar
1 teaspoon vanilla


Filling
8 oz pkg cream cheese
1 large egg [not ostrich]
1/3 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup choc chips
sugar


line muffin tins with paper cups.
 
Mix first 5 ingredients in large bowl.
Add next 4 ingredients and beat until well blended

To make filling, combine first 4 ingredients and beat well
Stir in choc. chips

Fill each muffin cup half full with batter
and top with 1 generous teaspoon of filling
Sprinkle with sugar

Bake @ 350 for 25 - 30 minutes
